Welcome to Sushi Huang Japanese Restaurant

  Dine in, Take out, All-You-Can-Eat

At Sushi Huang's we not only keeps it's traditional Japanese dishes but is also introducing sushi eaters to a more modern style Sushi, by creating new rolls and appetizers with ingredients from all over the world. To assure freshness of the fish, all fish are carefully picked daily by our most experienced chef. To ensure an affordable price and food quality for our customers we personally handpick all of our ingredients daily from the market directly, instead of relying on the local distributor. Our goal is to create a clean atmosphere and friendly service for friends and families to enjoy delicious and healthy Japanese dishes at an affordable price.

We encourage you to visit us and experience all that Sushi Huang to offer!
